Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Driim is developed in Sweden. This policy explains what we collect when you join the pre-release list, why, and the rights you have under the GDPR.

Information We Collect

When you join the pre-release list, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time you signed up

How We Use Your Information

We use your email address to:

  • Notify you about Driim's pre-release premier and launch
  • Send occasional updates related to dream work and the practice behind Driim

We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis

We process your email on the basis of your consent, given when you submit the form. You may withdraw that consent at any time — every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.

Data Storage

Sign-ups are handled by Driim's own systems and stored securely in our database, hosted in the EU. Confirmation and notification emails are sent through our email provider. We retain your email only as long as you remain on the list; if you leave, it is deleted or anonymized.

Joining is double opt-in: you confirm your email before your place is counted. If you were invited by a friend's link, we record that referral so we can credit them; that record is removed if you unsubscribe or ask us to delete your data.

Cookies

This landing page sets no advertising or tracking cookies. We store a small identifier in your browser only so we can show you your place in line on a return visit. Any analytics, if enabled, are limited to anonymous page views and traffic sources.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent and unsubscribe at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Sweden, the IMY)

Contact Us

For any question about this policy or your data, write to support@driim.app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a revised "last updated" date.
